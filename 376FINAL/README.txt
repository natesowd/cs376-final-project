The game uses up, down, left, right, and fire1 axises in the game.
Generally, if you are using keyboard and mouse - you can use WSAD to move and Space or left mouse to fire.
If you are using a console - you can use a joystick to move and A to fire.

The goal of the game is to fire and destroy as many enemies as possible while keeping youself alive.
You have 10 health points and getting bumped by one enemy will get one point reduced.
When your health reaches 0, you lose. 
You can restart the game by clicking on the restart buttom at the upper right corner.

To enter the game, you need to enter the Scene Menu.
